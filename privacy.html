Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Pensieve is built to forget. It collects nothing about you, has no accounts, and sends no data to us — because there is no "us" server to send it to.

The short version

Your notes live on your device. If you turn on iCloud sync, they sync through your own private iCloud account and nowhere else. Pensieve has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. The developer never sees, stores, or receives your notes.

What Pensieve stores

Pensieve stores only what you create and choose:

  • Your notes — the text you write, and timing details (when a note was created, when it expires, whether it is pinned). Stored locally in the app's private, sandboxed storage on your device.
  • Your settings — preferences like default note duration, appearance, and whether iCloud sync and app lock are enabled.

That is all. There is no name, email, account, contact list, location, or device identifier collected or required to use Pensieve.

iCloud sync (optional, off by default)

Pensieve does not sync anything unless you turn on iCloud sync in Settings. It is off by default.

When enabled, your notes sync across your own Apple devices using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) into your personal, private iCloud database. This data is stored in your iCloud account, protected by your Apple ID, and is not accessible to the developer of Pensieve. Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

You can turn sync off at any time in Settings. Turning it off stops future syncing; to remove already-synced data, delete the notes or sign the app out of iCloud from your device settings.

Biometric lock (optional)

If you enable app lock, Pensieve uses Face ID or Touch ID through Apple's system frameworks. Your biometric data is handled entirely by iOS and the Secure Enclave — it never leaves your device and is never seen or stored by Pensieve.

What Pensieve does not do

  • No accounts, sign-ups, or logins.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking.
  • No advertising and no ad identifiers.
  • No third-party SDKs, trackers, or data brokers.
  • No selling, sharing, or renting of any data — there is nothing collected to sell.

Data retention and deletion

Notes are designed to disappear. Unpinned notes expire on their own after their set duration and are permanently deleted. Deleted notes are held briefly in Trash and then removed for good. You can also delete any note at any time.

Deleting the Pensieve app removes all notes stored locally on that device. If you used iCloud sync, remove synced copies by deleting the notes or removing the app's data from your iCloud settings.

Children's privacy

Pensieve is not directed to children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
